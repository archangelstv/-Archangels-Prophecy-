The Future
This world is amid a drastic change. Many die, the blacks are no
more. The republicans all disappear. The Gays are non-existent and
many of the others are gone forever. Also, all the weapons everywhere
are gone along with those who have taken to them. The world’s religions
are a thing of the past as a one world religion engulfs the planet of the
future. Peace reins across the lands of Earth. It’s the Pandemic Covid 19.
Nothing can stop it. It continues to evolve across the face of this planet.
Day by day it continues until the world becomes a futuristic Phenom-
enon.
There are many other worlds. They do not intermingle with one an-
other. They keep to themselves for the most part, although they do talk
to one another and trade as well. Other human worlds take shape and
work together for the greater good.
More changes occur.
Ghost and demons like evil beings disappear from this realm. There
are no more hauntings. People begin to live much longer as the number
of are overcome. The new way of fighting them are already found. This
work amazingly well. This is part of the new future.
The Pandemic, Covid 19 is here for a reason. It is destroying this
old world and all the other being who live here. These others are not
human, contrary to what people think. They are far too different. If you
are part of these, you will simply begone forever. Nothing will be done
to save you. as only humans will survive to live in this new age.
